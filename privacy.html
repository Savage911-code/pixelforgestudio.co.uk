Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what information PixelForge Studio ("we", "us") collects when you visit this website or get in touch, and how we use it. We've kept it short because we collect very little.

Who we are

PixelForge Studio is a UK-based web design service. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at hello@pixelforgestudio.co.uk.

What we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, the service you're enquiring about, your budget range, and your project details. We don't collect any information automatically beyond standard, anonymous web server logs (e.g. for security purposes).

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide a quote or proposal.
  • To communicate with you about a project you've asked us about.
  • We will never sell your information or use it for marketing without your consent.

How your enquiry is processed

Our contact form is delivered using FormSubmit, a third-party form processing service that forwards submissions to our email inbox. FormSubmit acts as a data processor for this purpose only — see their privacy policy for details.

Cookies & fonts

This site does not use tracking or analytics cookies. We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may involve a request to Google's servers when the page loads.

Data retention

We keep enquiry emails for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you and, if you become a client, for the duration of our working relationship plus a reasonable period for accounting purposes.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@pixelforgestudio.co.uk and we'll respond promptly.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
